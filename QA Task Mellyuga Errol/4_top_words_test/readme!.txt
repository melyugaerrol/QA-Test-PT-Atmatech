click_top_word
	Displays the words that are often discussed and also the total words in the topic.
	If we click one of the 'words', it will display a twitter post that writes the 'word'
	There is a filter to select Twitter posts that contain the selected 'word' based on :
	1. Recent tweets
	2. Oldest tweets
	3. Popular tweets
	4. Most liked tweets
	5. Most replied tweets
	6. Most retweeted
