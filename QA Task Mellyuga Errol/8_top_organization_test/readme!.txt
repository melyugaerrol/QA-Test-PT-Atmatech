top_organization
	Displays mentioned organization or organization accounts that are often discussed and also total of the 
	organization in the topic.
	If we click one of the 'organization', will display the organization being mentioned or organization account who posted the tweet.
	There is a filter to select Twitter posts that contain the selected 'organization' based on :
	1. Recent tweets
	2. Oldest tweets
	3. Popular tweets
	4. Most liked tweets
	5. Most replied tweets
	6. Most retweeted
