top_people
	Displays peoples or accounts that are often discussed and also total of the mentioned people or accounts in the topic.
	If we click one of the 'people', will display the person being mentioned or the person who posted the tweet.
	There is a filter to select Twitter posts that contain the selected 'people' based on :
	1. Recent tweets
	2. Oldest tweets
	3. Popular tweets
	4. Most liked tweets
	5. Most replied tweets
	6. Most retweeted
